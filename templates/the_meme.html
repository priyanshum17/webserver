The Meme: Rickrolling

Rickrolling is an internet meme involving an unexpected appearance of the music video for Rick Astley's 1987 song "Never Gonna Give You Up," usually by way of a disguised hyperlink.

Origins of the Meme

The first known Rickroll occurred in 2007 on the imageboard 4chan, where a link to the music video was disguised as a link to a trailer for the video game Grand Theft Auto IV. The prank quickly spread to other websites and platforms.

How it Works

A Rickroll typically involves a user being tricked into clicking a link that they believe will lead to relevant content, but instead, it redirects them to the "Never Gonna Give You Up" music video. The unexpected nature of the redirection is key to the humor of the prank.

Cultural Impact

Rickrolling became a widespread internet phenomenon, transcending online communities and entering mainstream culture. It has been used in various contexts, from sporting events to political protests, demonstrating its pervasive and enduring appeal. Rick Astley himself has embraced the meme, often participating in Rickrolls during his live performances.
